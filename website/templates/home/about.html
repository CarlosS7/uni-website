The English Language Enhancement Center (ELEC), at Payap University, offers English Language courses to help you develop your communication skills in the international language.

We offer:

  • Intensive English programs for students who want to study in English at university.
  • Teacher training - TESOL certificate courses.
  • IELTS / TOEFL preparation courses.
  • Entrance exams every Friday.
  • A self-access center, where you can improve your English in a fun and relaxed environment.
  • Other English courses according to your needs.

Entrance exam for future Payap International College students

For students who want to study at the Payap International College, and who are not native speakers, we provide an entrance exam every Friday at 10:00 in the morning.

The exam lasts three hours and has four parts:

  • Listening
  • Structure (grammar)
  • Reading
  • Writing

The score you need to pass the exam depends on the course you are applying for.

Please remember that the exam starts at 10:00 am. If you are late, you will not be able to get a good score.
