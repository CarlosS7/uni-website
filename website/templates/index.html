Entrance exam for future Payap International College students

For students who want to study at the Payap International College, and who are not native speakers, we provide an entrance exam every Friday at 10:00 in the morning.

The exam lasts three hours and has four parts:

  • Listening
  • Structure (grammar)
  • Reading
  • Writing

The score you need to pass the exam depends on the course you are applying for.

Please remember that the exam starts at 10:00 am. If you are late, you will not be able to get a good score.

Courses

Intensive English Program

Ideal for students who want to study a university course in English

An intensive 180-hour course which gives you a great chance to become a lot better at English.

There are three levels, elementary, intermediate and advanced, and it takes one semester to complete each level. Students who complete the advanced level will have learned the skills they need to successfully study in English at university level.

A TOEFL, IELTS or Payap Proficiency Test score and/or an oral interview are used to work out the applicant’s English level in order to place the student at the most beneficial level of instruction. Students are expected to attend all classes in which they are placed.

To apply for the Intensive English Program course, call us, email us, or come in to our office any time from Monday to Friday 8:30 am to 4:30 pm.

Alternatively, you can send us an application form.

TESOL Certificate

Teacher training for both native and nonnative speakers

The TESOL Certificate is an internationally recognized qualification for teachers of English.

We offer 120-hour TESOL Certificate courses for English teachers. The Certificate in TESOL is a program designed for graduates with near-native proficiency in English who want to begin a teaching career. It is taught entirely in English, and provides the basic training for teaching English as a non-native language.

The program is a four-week intensive program, which covers the structure of spoken and written texts in English, contemporary approaches to language teaching and evaluation, and classroom practice.

To apply for the TESOL Certificate course, call us, email us, or come in to our office any time from Monday to Friday 8:30 am to 4:30 pm.

Alternatively, you can send us an application form.

IELTS / TOEFL Preparation

For students who want to study abroad

For students who want to study abroad and need to prepare for an IELTS, or TOEFL, test, we also offer IELTS / TOEFL preparation courses.

There is no fixed schedule for these courses, but if you are interested in taking an IELTS, or TOEFL, preparation course, please sign up, and we can contact you when the course starts. A minimum of four students is needed to start an IELTS / TOEFL preparation course.

To apply for the IELTS / TOEFL preparation course, call us, email us, or come in to our office any time from Monday to Friday 8:30 am to 4:30 pm.

Notes for students

Overseas students

  • All fees must be paid in full before we can send you a letter for your visa application.
  • We charge 1,500 THB for postage of documentation.
  • The visa application process can take several weeks, so please book in advance.

Students currently in Thailand

  • All students must take an LEC placement test (200 Baht) before entering any program.
  • Registration and payment are compulsory before any program commences.
  • The LEC reserves the right not to open classes if fewer than 4 students enroll.
  • Book fees are not included in the course fees but photocopies are.
  • The LEC reserves the right to change course fees and dates without prior notice.
  • No refunds are given once the semester has started except in very exceptional circumstances
  • If a refund is permitted there will be a charge of 1,000 Baht to cover administration costs.
